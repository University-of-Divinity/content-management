Student Surveys

During your studies, you'll have regular opportunities to have a say in your learning experience, through the Student Unit Evaluation online survey. We are committed to listening to your feedback and informing you of the results as we improve unit content and delivery.

The Semester 1 survey opportunity is coming up shortly. You will receive a link in ARK to a survey on your experience in your units. Keep an eye out for emails through ARK from 31 May which will let you know when the survey is available for your unit.

In the comments section of the survey, you can take the opportunity to give us feedback in relation to your experience of transitioning and thriving in online learning during the Coronavirus restrictions. For example, what was your learning experience in comparison to a face-to-face classroom? Did you have access to the resources you need on ARK and the Library Hub? Did the experience of online learning meet your expectations in communication and interactivity with staff and students?

Code of Conduct

The University of Divinity has a new Code of Conduct. The Code of Conduct applies to all members of the University when on University or College premises, using University or College facilities and services, or engaging in University or College activities related to their duties and responsibilities as a member of the University. The new Code of Conduct is available on the University website. A short video of the Vice-Chancellor introducing the Code is also available.
